## 16:30ŌĆō21:30 ŌĆö Formal safer defaults

**Teaching point:** When unsure, choose the respectful/simple version.

**On-screen table:**

| Risky/casual | Safer formal default |
|---|---|
| Hey! | Guten Tag. |
| Was? | Noch einmal, bitte. |
| Hm? | Entschuldigung? |
| Bye! | Auf Wiedersehen. |

**Presenter:**

[Boss explains in Malayalam/Manglish: This is not about being stiff. It is about not sounding rude when your German is still small.]

**Repair drill:**

Presenter shows risky line. Learner says safer line.

1. `Hey!` ŌåÆ **Guten Tag.**
2. `Was?` ŌåÆ **Noch einmal, bitte.**
3. `Bye!` ŌåÆ **Auf Wiedersehen.**
4. no thank-you after help ŌåÆ **Danke.**
