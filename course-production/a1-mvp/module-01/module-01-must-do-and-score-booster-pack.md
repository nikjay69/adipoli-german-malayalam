# Module 1 · Must-Do and Score-Booster Practice Pack

Updated: 2026-06-02 15:20 CEST

**Module:** First German Moment

**Purpose:** Give the learner exact prescribed tasks after/between videos. No resource dumping. Every task has order, output, and time box.

**Publishing note:** Public-resource links are not verified in this run. Curation slots are included as `LINK NEEDED — verify before publishing` rather than fake links.

---

## Module 1 required outcome

By the end of Module 1, learner can:

```text
Hear and answer a first formal greeting.
Say: Guten Morgen, Frau Fischer.
Say/write: Ich lerne Deutsch.
Ask: Noch einmal, bitte. / Langsam, bitte.
Leave: Auf Wiedersehen.
```

Closed Module 1 checkpoint comes after Lesson 7.

## Must-do path, exact order

### 1. First audio reply

- **When:** Before Lesson 1 / first mission.
- **Task:** Play Frau Fischer greeting once. Answer aloud.
- **Input:** `Frau Fischer: Guten Morgen.`
- **Output:** Learner says `Guten Morgen, Frau Fischer.`
- **Time box:** 3m.
- **Weakness tag if failed:** `sprechen:greeting_reply`, `hoeren:greetings`.

### 2. Lesson 1 practice — answer Frau Fischer

- **When:** After Lesson 1.
- **Task:** Shadow 5 lines, then answer 3 teacher prompts.
- **Output:** Learner says:
  1. `Guten Morgen, Frau Fischer.`
  2. `Ja. Ich lerne Deutsch.`
  3. `Auf Wiedersehen.`
- **Time box:** 12m.
- **Pass:** completes all 3 aloud.

### 3. First writing memory

- **When:** After Lesson 1 practice.
- **Task:** Hide the model. Type/write one sentence from memory.
- **Output:** `Ich lerne Deutsch.`
- **Time box:** 5m.
- **Pass:** sentence understandable; `Ich` and `Deutsch` capitalised.
- **Weakness tag if failed:** `schreiben:first_sentence`.

### 4. Lesson 2 path check

- **When:** After Lesson 2.
- **Task:** Say the course path and one personal reason.
- **Output:**
  - Says: `Watch → Listen → Say → Check → Fix`.
  - Says in English/Manglish: “I am learning German for ____.”
- **Time box:** 6m.
- **Pass:** reason chosen; no resource-browsing confusion.

### 5. Lesson 3 sound drill

- **When:** After Lesson 3.
- **Task:** Shadow the 10-line sound set slowly.
- **Output:** Learner says all 10 lines:
  1. `Ich.`
  2. `Ich lerne Deutsch.`
  3. `Guten Morgen, Frau Fischer.`
  4. `Auf Wiedersehen.`
  5. `Tschüss.`
  6. `Bitte.`
  7. `Danke schön.`
  8. `Noch einmal, bitte.`
  9. `Langsam, bitte.`
  10. `Das ist schön.`
- **Time box:** 15m.
- **Pass:** completes all lines; flags hardest sound.
- **Weakness tags:** `pronunciation:ch_sch`, `pronunciation:w_v`, `pronunciation:umlaut`.

### 6. Lesson 3 discrimination check

- **When:** Immediately after sound drill.
- **Task:** Choose correct sound for 8 audio/text pairs.
- **Output:** 6/8 correct.
- **Time box:** 6m.
- **Items:** `ich/sch`, `Weber`, `Tschüss`, `schön`, `Wasser`, `Visum`, `Deutsch`, `Auf Wiedersehen`.

### 7. Lesson 4 greeting-context drill

- **When:** After Lesson 4.
- **Task:** Answer 8 time/context prompts aloud.
- **Output:**
  1. 9 AM teacher → `Guten Morgen, Frau Fischer.`
  2. 2 PM examiner → `Guten Tag.`
  3. 7 PM class → `Guten Abend.`
  4. leaving office → `Auf Wiedersehen.`
  5. friend message → `Hallo.`
  6. leaving friend → `Tschüss.`
  7. unknown adult counter → `Guten Tag.`
  8. teacher goodbye → `Auf Wiedersehen, Frau Fischer.`
- **Time box:** 10m.
- **Pass:** 7/8 context choices correct.
- **Weakness tags:** `hoeren:greetings`, `sprechen:formality`, `vocab:greeting_set`.

### 8. Lesson 5 politeness repair

- **When:** After Lesson 5.
- **Task:** Replace rough/casual lines with safer polite German.
- **Output:**
  - Help received → `Danke.`
  - Need repetition → `Noch einmal, bitte.`
  - Need to interrupt → `Entschuldigung.`
  - Formal leaving → `Auf Wiedersehen.`
  - Reply to thanks → `Bitte.`
- **Time box:** 8m.
- **Pass:** 5/5 correct or repeats until correct.
- **Weakness tag:** `sprechen:formality`, `vocab:greeting_set`.

### 9. Lesson 6 repair phrase role-play

- **When:** After Lesson 6.
- **Task:** Choose repeat vs slow-down phrase and say it aloud.
- **Output:**
  - Missed line → `Noch einmal, bitte.`
  - Too fast → `Langsam, bitte.`
  - Formal goodbye → `Auf Wiedersehen.`
  - Casual goodbye → `Tschüss.`
- **Time box:** 8m.
- **Pass:** 4/4 context choices correct.
- **Weakness tag:** `sprechen:request_phrase`, `sprechen:formality`.

### 10. Lesson 7 full mini-conversation

- **When:** After Lesson 7.
- **Task:** Perform learner lines aloud with only Frau Fischer prompts shown/played.
- **Output:**
  ```text
  Guten Morgen, Frau Fischer.
  Noch einmal, bitte.
  Ja. Ich lerne Deutsch.
  Danke.
  Auf Wiedersehen, Frau Fischer.
  ```
- **Time box:** 12m.
- **Pass:** completes without reading full transcript; repair phrase allowed.
- **Weakness tags:** `sprechen:greeting_reply`, `sprechen:formality`, `hoeren:question_recognition`.

### 11. Module 1 closed checkpoint

- **When:** After all videos/practice.
- **Task:** Complete checkpoint in `module-01-checkpoint-rubric.md`.
- **Output:** score + weakness tags + exact recovery.
- **Time box:** 25m.
- **Pass:** 70%+, no Hören+Sprechen double fail, at least one spoken reply completed.

## Must-do recovery cards

### Weakness: `hoeren:greetings`

- **Do:** Replay 10 greeting audio lines without subtitles. Choose/say response.
- **Output:** 8/10 correct.
- **Time box:** 12m.
- **Retest:** 5 new lines; pass 4/5.

### Weakness: `sprechen:greeting_reply`

- **Do:** Repeat three target replies 5 times each.
- **Output:** Say once without looking:
  - `Guten Morgen, Frau Fischer.`
  - `Ja. Ich lerne Deutsch.`
  - `Auf Wiedersehen.`
- **Time box:** 10m.
- **Retest:** one teacher prompt exchange.

### Weakness: `sprechen:formality`

- **Do:** Sort 8 contexts into formal/casual; say safer answer.
- **Output:** 7/8 correct.
- **Time box:** 8m.
- **Retest:** 4 formal contexts.

### Weakness: `pronunciation:ch_sch`

- **Do:** Shadow `ich`, `Deutsch`, `Tschüss`, `schön` slowly.
- **Output:** record/say 8 words; identify `ich` vs `isch` in 8 pairs.
- **Time box:** 15m.
- **Retest:** 5 words.

### Weakness: `pronunciation:w_v`

- **Do:** Compare `Weber`, `Wasser`, `Visum`, `viel`.
- **Output:** say 6 words with German `w` awareness.
- **Time box:** 10m.
- **Retest:** 6-word read-aloud.

### Weakness: `schreiben:first_sentence`

- **Do:** Copy `Ich lerne Deutsch.` twice. Hide. Type from memory.
- **Output:** one clean sentence.
- **Time box:** 8m.
- **Retest:** one dictation.

## Score-booster tasks

Use only after pass/weak-pass or if learner explicitly wants extra confidence. Assign one at a time.

### Booster 1 — pronunciation confidence

- **Target:** `pronunciation:ch_sch`, `pronunciation:umlaut`.
- **Resource:** LINK NEEDED — verify one short public German pronunciation video before publishing.
- **Task:** Watch only the selected 8–12m segment. Then record/say 8 words: `ich`, `Deutsch`, `schön`, `Tschüss`, `Weber`, `Wasser`, `spät`, `Mädchen`.
- **Time box:** 15m.
- **Output:** 8-word recording/self-mark.

### Booster 2 — greeting listening stamina

- **Target:** `hoeren:greetings`.
- **Resource:** Adipoli internal slow audio set preferred. Public link optional: LINK NEEDED — verify before publishing.
- **Task:** Listen to 20 greeting/politeness lines. Write or choose the response.
- **Time box:** 15m.
- **Output:** 16/20 correct.

### Booster 3 — Goethe micro speaking card

- **Target:** `sprechen:formality`, `sprechen:greeting_reply`.
- **Resource:** Adipoli card set; no public link needed.
- **Task:** Examiner greets or asks one first question. Learner answers formally.
- **Cards:**
  1. Examiner: `Guten Tag.` → learner replies.
  2. Examiner: `Lernen Sie Deutsch?` → learner replies.
  3. Examiner speaks too fast → learner asks repair.
  4. Examiner ends → learner thanks and leaves.
- **Time box:** 10m.
- **Output:** four spoken replies.

### Booster 4 — writing accuracy

- **Target:** `schreiben:first_sentence`.
- **Resource:** Adipoli internal worksheet.
- **Task:** Write the first sentence and three copied greeting lines neatly.
- **Output:**
  ```text
  Ich lerne Deutsch.
  Guten Morgen.
  Danke.
  Auf Wiedersehen.
  ```
- **Time box:** 8m.
