# Adipoli German — Cinematic Video Series

## Kuttan's Journey

A cinematic story series following **Kuttan**, a young Malayali man from Kerala who decides to learn German and move to Germany for a nursing career.

---

## Series Arc

| Episode | Title | Summary |
|---------|-------|---------|
| 1 | The Spark | Kuttan discovers a Malayali guy in Germany on his phone, replays it, decides to start learning German |
| 2 | The Decision | Kuttan decides to go to Germany, prepares documents, says goodbye to family, boards the plane from Kerala |
| 3 | Preparing for Germany | Kuttan studies German, saves money, gets documents ready, has farewell dinner, leaves home |

## Kuttan Character Lock

### Visual Identity
- young Malayali man, early 20s
- warm, expressive face
- medium-brown skin tone
- black hair, clean and simple
- no beard, no glasses
- not hyper-stylized or goofy
- not realistic live-action
- **Pixar-style 3D**, clean and emotionally readable

### Outfit
- casual white cotton shirt
- jeans
- rubber slippers (at home)
- small black backpack (travel)

### Emotional state
- curious, restless
- quietly pulled toward something bigger
- not dramatic from frame one
- resolution grows gradually

## Animation Prompts — General Rules

### Always include
- Pixar-style 3D animation
- Kuttan identity locked
- warm cinematic lighting unless scene requires otherwise
- no text overlays, subtitles, or watermark

### Always exclude
- hat, glasses, beard
- realistic style, live action, anime
- scene redesign, camera jump
- face morphing, shirt color change

### For phone shots
- phone content must be **meaningful story content**, not blank glow
- phone screen should show: a confident Malayali guy in Germany in reel/video format

### Motion guidelines
- keep movement subtle and natural
- small breathing, tiny head adjustments
- no complex hand choreography
- no crowd interactions
- no wild camera moves
