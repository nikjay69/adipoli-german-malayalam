## Narrative Rules

1. **Phone/app is always present** — it's the constant companion from Kerala to Germany
2. **Device variety**: phone (casual), tablet (study), laptop (university/work)
3. **No scene repeats the same setting + emotion** — always something new
4. **Each video works standalone** as a social media clip AND as part of the series
5. **Character consistency**: Gemini Flash + V1 reference image for all stills
6. **Fresh seeds for face shots, chain only for camera moves (1 hop max)**
7. **Kerala warmth ↔ German gray** — the visual contrast tells the story
8. **The course is the hero** — it enables everything, but subtly, not as an ad
