### Script Approach
- Frame as "Level up!" content — never make learners feel bad for skipping
- Malayalam bridge is CRITICAL here because grammar concepts are harder
- Example-heavy, rule-light
- Each script starts with "Why this matters in real German" (motivation before mechanics)

### Special Note: weil-clauses
- Already partially covered in Module 12 (v12-4-2)
- Module 16 extends to dass + wenn clauses
- Malayalam parallel: verb-final word order is NATURAL in Malayalam — leverage this!

## Why These Modules Still Matter (Even If Optional)

1. **Retention:** Students who reach Module 15-16 are deeply engaged — rewarding them with quality content prevents drop-off
2. **A2 bridge:** Module 16 grammar is the foundation of A2 — students who do it will have an easier time continuing
3. **Real-life value:** Module 15 cultural content prevents social mistakes that textbooks don't cover
4. **Marketing:** "18 modules including culture and advanced grammar" sounds more premium than "14 modules"

### But the constraint is clear:
**Zero time should go to optional modules before exam-critical modules (1–14, 17–18) are production-exercise-ready with audio.**
