> **A1 Rule:** Kuttan does NOT reach Germany in A1.

> **Arc Rule:** Kuttan's German visibly improves from Video 1 to Video 15.

---

## Kuttan — Character Reference (consistent across ALL Veo prompts)

**Kuttan** — young Malayali man, 24–26 years old.
- Warm brown skin, dark slightly-curly hair
- Bright, curious eyes — eager but nervous energy
- Lean build, average height
- **Early videos (1–6):** casual Kerala style — simple cotton shirt, lungi or jeans, rubber slippers
- **Mid videos (7–12):** slightly smarter — collared shirt, jeans, shoes
- **Late videos (13–15):** neat formal shirt, clean shoes — exam-ready
- Always carries a small black backpack
- Small notebook that appears increasingly filled with German words

---

## German Progression Key

| Videos | Kuttan's German | What it sounds like |
|--------|----------------|---------------------|
| 1–2 | Zero German | Doesn't attempt it |
| 3–4 | First broken attempts | "Gu... Guten Tag?" with a laugh |
| 5–6 | Short phrases, wrong stress | "Ich bin... Kuttan. Ja?" |
| 7–8 | Simple sentences, grammar errors | "Ich möchte ein... the ticket?" |
| 9–10 | More confident, self-correcting | Starts a sentence, fixes it mid-way |
| 11–12 | Mostly correct, occasional slip | Natural rhythm emerging |
| 13–14 | Exam-ready, composed | Answers clearly, small smile after |
| 15 | Clean, confident, earned | No hesitation |

> These German moments appear as **subtitle text overlays** in the AI video.
> The video itself is silent or ambient — no spoken dialogue generated.
