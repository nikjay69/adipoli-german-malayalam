**Topic: Afternoon (Der Nachmittag)**

**Topic: Night Routine (Die Nacht)**
- "Ich gehe ins Bett." (I go to bed).
- "Ich schlafe um 22 Uhr." (I sleep at 10 PM).
- "Gute Nacht!" (Good night!).
