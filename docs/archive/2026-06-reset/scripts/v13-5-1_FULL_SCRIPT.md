## Script

**Narration (Malayalam/Manglish):**
Namskaram machane! Aadyathe lessons-ul nammal home search, viewing, and agreement (Mietvertrag) padichu. Ini, actual "Moving In" (Einzug) and "Key Handover" (Schlüsselübergabe) patti samsarikkaam. Ithanu final process of apartment acquisition.

**Topic: Key Handover (Schlüsselübergabe)**
Agreement sign cheythittu physical keys (Schlüssel) nammalkku kittum.
- "der Schlüssel" (key / chavi). Usually standard apartments have 3 keys for each door (Entrance, Apartment, Cellar).
- "Haben Sie alle Schlüssel?" (Do you have all keys?).
- "Das ist der Schlüssel für die Briefkästen." (Letter box key).

**Topic: Transfer Protocol (Übergabeprotokoll)**
Ithu super critical aayirikkum. Germany-yil oru room-e keriyaal physically details check cheyyanam.
- "Übergabeprotokoll" (Transfer protocol / status report). 
Vermieter-um ningallum koodi room walkthrough cheyyum. Ella items-um existing damage-ukkal (floor scratches, wall issues, etc.) document cheyyanam. Illenkil, empty aakumbol ningal "athunde aayirunnu" ennu parayunnathu accept cheyyaan chance kuravaayirikkum. Use your phone to take photos!

"Gibt es Mängel?" (Are there defects? / Enthenkilum pottalum pookalum?).
- "Die Wand ist schmutzig." (The wall is dirty).
- "Das Fenster schließt nicht richtig." (The window doesn't close correctly).

**Topic: Utilities Status**
Transfer time-ul meters (Counter readings) check cheyyanam.
- "der Stromzähler" (electricity meter).
- "der Wasserzähler" (water meter).
- "der Gaszähler" (gas meter).

Ithu check-in numbers agreement or protocol-il ezhuthi vekkuka. Ithu base cheythaanu ningalkku electricity or gas billing thudangunnath. (Check the numbers carefully, machane!).

**Topic: Moving In (Einzug)**
- "Ich ziehe am 1. März ein." (I m moving in on March 1st).
- "Benötigen Sie Hilfe beim Umzug?" (Do you need help with the move?).
- "Ich bestelle einen Transporter." (I am ordering a van / Vandi vilikanam).

Helpful tip: Germany-yil "Umzugshelfer" (move helpers) small company-ukal pole thaanu. Friends-ine invite cheyyaanum parayaam: "Kannst du mir beim Umzug helfen?" (Can you help me with the move?). If yes, bring some Pizza and Beer as "Umzug-reward"!

**Topic: Address Registration (Anmeldung)**
Moving in kazhinjal 2 weeks-ul "Bürgerbüro" (City office)-il address change notification (Anmeldung) cheyyanam. Ithanu residence status notification. Vermieter tharum "Wohnungsgeberbestätigung" (Landlord's confirmation). 
"Ich möchte mich anmelden." (I want to register).

**Topic: Internet and Post**
Post-office-un "Nachsendeauftrag" (forwarding order) or local post setup change notification ezhuthunnathe common aanu. Router connection broadband etc. advance cheythaale move-in day-yil internet kite pattu. Don't forget, speed is everything!

**Closing:**
Keys, transfer protocol, meter readings, and registration — ee final steps acquisition process ippo ningalkku ariyaam. Home acquisition complete path set aayi! Module 13 is finished. 

Adutha module-il nammal "Life in Germany (A1+ Culture)" patti samsarikkaam. Stay home, stay safe! Bye!
