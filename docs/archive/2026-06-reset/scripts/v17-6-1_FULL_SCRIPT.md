## Script

**Narration (Malayalam/Manglish):**
Namskaram machane! Aadyathe lessons-ul nammal Hören and Lesen components (Teil 1, 2, 3) patti padichu. Ini, "Final Mock Exam" (Purna pariksha practice) patti samsarikkaam. Ithu actual Goethe A1 exam format-ul valare essential aayirikkum.

**Topic: Hören Mock Section (3 Parts)**
- **Teil 1**: Short everyday messages.
Announcement: "Der Zug nach München hat Verspätung." 
Statement: "Der Zug nach München fährt pünktlich." → **Falsch**.

- **Teil 2**: Dialogues with multiple choice (a/b/c).
Dialogue: "Ich möchte ein Kilo Äpfel." "Hier, bitte sehr. Noch etwas?" 
Question: What is he buying? (Apples).

- **Teil 3**: Public announcements matched to situations.
Announcement: Radio news about weather. 
Situation: You want to know the weather for tomorrow.

**Topic: Lesen Mock Section (3 Parts)**
- **Teil 1**: Short texts/signs.
Sign: "Eingang nur für Mitarbeiter." (Entrance only for employees).
Question: Can you enter? (No).

- **Teil 2**: Ads and classifieds.
Ad: Apartment for 500 Euro warm rent. 
Question: Is the heating included? (Yes).

- **Teil 3**: Short emails/letters.
Email from a colleague about a meeting.
Question: At what time is the meeting? (10 Uhr).

**Topic: Exam Day Tips (Last Minute Checklist)**
- **Read carefully**: Every word in the question matters! (Especially "nicht", "kein").
- **Time Management**: One minute per question in Lesen is great!
- **Elimination**: Wrong options-ukal cross-out cheyyaam.

"Viel Glück!" (Good luck! / Adiyipoli aayittu ezhuthu!).

**Topic: Final Interaction Practice**
Machane, ippo njane oru model exam flow introduce cheyyaam.
"Sprechen Sie langsam?" (Hören Teil 2).
"Ist das richtig oder falsch?" (Lesen Teil 1).
"Wo ist die Lösung?" (Solution find cheyyanam).

Adholoke! Simple natural exam flow.
