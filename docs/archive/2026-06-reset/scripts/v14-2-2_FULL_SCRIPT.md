## Script

**Narration (Malayalam/Manglish):**
Namaskaram machane! Namukku ippo German work culture-inte basic rules ariyam. Punctuality and hierarchy okke nammal padichu. Ini, actual workplace-il enthanu nadakkunnu ennu nokkaam. Germany-yil work cheyyumbol, especially "Soft Skills" valare important aanu. Innu nammal athu pattiyaanu samsarikkan pokunnathe.

Sadharna nammal Kerala-yil "Yes" parayunnathe polle alla Germany-yil. Directness aanu avide key!

**Topic: Directness (Direktheit)**
"Directness" ennu parayumbol, kure peru karuthum ithu "rude" aayittulla behavior aano ennu. Alla! In Germany, efficiency-kku aanu high priority. Athu kondu, enthenkilum karyam parayumbol, sutheerayaayittu, direct aayittu parayanam. Sugaring and buttering okke avide kuravaanu. "I will try" ennu nammal parayumbol, oru German-u manasilakunnatha "he will do it" ennaanu. Half-commitments avide work aavilla.

So, enthenkilum karyam ningalkku cheyyaan pattillennu thonniyaal, direct aayittu "Nein, das kann ich leider nicht machen" (No, I unfortunately cannot do that) ennu parayunnu. Athinu aviru respect tharum. Direct aayittu feedback kodukkunnathe oru negative aayittu avare kaanilla.

**Topic: Teamwork**
Aduthathu aanu Teamwork. "Teamfähigkeit" (Team-ability). German companies are usually horizontal machines. Ella kollaag-inteyum objective "getting the job done" ennathe aanu.

If someone corrects your German or your work, don't take it personally! Athu avaru team-inte betterment-nu vendi parayunnathaanu. "Feedback Culture" valare strong aanu. 

**Topic: Reliability (Zuverlässigkeit)**
"Zuverlässigkeit" — Reliable aayirikkuka. Oralkku oru vakku koduthaal, athu paalikkanam. Germany-yil trusted employee aakan, high level skills-ne kaalum avaru munnil vekkunnathu reliable aano ennathe aanu.

"Can you finish this by Friday?" 
If you say "Ja", friday evening-nu munnil athu avante table-il undayirikkanam. Friday varumbol "Sir, pinne..." ennu parayunnathu valare bad impression undakkum.

**Topic: Small Talk**
Nammude Kerala-yil office-il chennal aadyam "Kaappi kudicho?", "Family okke engane?" ennu chodhikkunna oru culture undallo. Germany-yil work-um private life-um separate aanu (Work-Life-Separation).

Early morning small talk valare kuravaayirikkum. Ellavarum avavante desk-il irunnu work cheyyum. Oru 10 min break-ilo lunch-ilo mathram aanu small talk. Athu kondu "nammule aarum mind cheyyunnillallo" ennu vichaarichu irikkaruthe! Athu avante focused work culture-inte bhaagam aanu.

If someone asks "Wie war dein Wochenende?" (How was your weekend?), short aayittu utheram parayuka. "Es war gut, danke. Und dein?" (It was good, thanks. And yours?). Direct work-ilekku kadakkum!

**Closing:**
So, directness, teamwork, and reliability — ee moonu karyangal work culture-il manasilakkiyaal German job-il ningal king aayirikkum! 

Adutha lesson-il nammal "Public Transport" patti padikkum. Until then, tschüss!
