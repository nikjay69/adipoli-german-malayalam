## Section 5: Time Expression Glue (6:30–7:30)
### Visual: [Clean list — NOT a massive table. Group by type]
### Narration:

"Time expressions are the GLUE connecting your verbs. Three types:

**Type 1 — Time of day (add -s!):**
morgenS, mittagS, abendS, nachtS.
⏸️ Pattern: just add -s to the time of day!

**Type 2 — Specific times:**
'um 7 Uhr' — at 7
'von 8 bis 5' — from 8 to 5
'gegen 18 Uhr' — around 6 PM

**Type 3 — Sequencing:**
'nach der Arbeit' — after work
'vor dem Frühstück' — before breakfast

⏸️ Quick test: How do you say 'after work' in German? → 'nach der Arbeit'

[EXAM-TIP] These time words appear constantly in Hören. When you hear 'morgens', 'um halb drei', 'nach dem Mittagessen' — your brain should instantly snap to the time context!

🎬 [Energy: rapid-fire, useful, don't linger]"
