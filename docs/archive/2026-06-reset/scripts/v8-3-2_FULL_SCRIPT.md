## Section 2: Room & Size — Zi. and qm (0:50–2:30)

### Visual: [HIGHLIGHT: 1. "Zi." = Zimmer | 2. "Whg" = Wohnung | 3. "qm" = Quadratmeter. Chart: 60 qm ≈ 650 sq.ft.]
### Narration: [ENERGY: steady, instructional]
"The first thing you look for: How big is it?

[Terms appear]
- 🔊 **'Wohnung'** (Whg) — *Apartment.*
- 🔊 **'Zimmer'** (Zi.) — *Rooms. (Note: A 2-room apartment is 1 living room + 1 bedroom. The kitchen and bath are extra!)*
- 🔊 **'Quadratmeter'** (qm) — *Square meters. This is the floor space.*

*Zi. (Zimmer) ennu parayum-apol nammal bathroom count cheyyilla. 'Wohnung' (Whg) orkkanam!*

⏸️ **Try: A 3-room apartment?**
### Practice Pause: ⏸️ (2 seconds)
**3-Zi. Wohnung.** ✅"

## Section 3: The Floor — EG and OG (2:30–4:30)

### Visual: [DIAGRAM: Building floors. EG (Ground) → 1. OG (1st) → 2. OG (2nd) → DG (Attic/Dachgeschoss).]
### Narration: [ENERGY: building, logical]
"Now, what floor is it on?

[Abbreviations]
- 🔊 **'EG'** (Erdgeschoss) — *Ground floor. (Erde = Earth).*
- 🔊 **'OG'** (Obergeschoss) — *Upper floor. (Ober = Above).*
- 🔊 **'DG'** (Dachgeschoss) — *Attic/Roof floor. (Dach = Roof).*

*Note: In Germany, the ground floor is 0 (EG). The floor above it is the 1st floor (1. OG).*

⏸️ **Try: The ground floor.**
### Practice Pause: ⏸️ (2 seconds)
**Erdgeschoss (EG).** ✅"

## Section 4: The Features — EBK and NK (4:30–6:30)

### Visual: [ICONS: Kitchen (EBK) | Bills (NK) | Balcony (Balk). Caption: "What's included?".]
### Narration: [ENERGY: building, logical]
"Next, the bells and whistles.

[Terms appear]
- 🔊 **'EBK'** (Einbauküche) — *Fitted kitchen. This is a big plus in Germany!*
- 🔊 **'NK'** (Nebenkosten) — *Side costs (water, heating, trash).*
- 🔊 **'Balkon'** (Balk.) — *Balcony.*

⏸️ **Try: Fitted kitchen.**

## Section 5: The Price — Warm vs. Kalt (6:30–8:00)

### Visual: [TABLE: Kaltmiete (Base) vs. Warmmiete (Total). Formula: Kalt + NK = Warm. Ice icon for Kalt, Flame icon for Warm.]
### Narration: [ENERGY: cultural, structural]
"This is the most important part. Don't get tricked!

[The Rent Rule]
1. 🔊 **'Kaltmiete'** — *Base rent. (Just the walls).*
2. 🔊 **'Warmmiete'** — *Total rent. (Walls + Heating + Water).*

*Kaltmiete plus Nebenkosten equals Warmmiete. Always check 'Warm' rent for your budget!*

[EXAM-TIP] In the **Lesen** (reading) exam, they might ask: 'Was ist der Gesamtpreis?' (What's the total price?). Look for the word **'warm'**!"
