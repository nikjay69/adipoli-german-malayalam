## Script

**Narration (Malayalam/Manglish):**
Namskaram machane! Innu nammalkku oru "Emergency" (Notfall) situation-il enthanu cheyyunnathu ennu nokkaam. Ithu aarkkum vendaatha oru situation aanu, but Germany-yil jeevikkan ee words and numbers ningalkku absolute sense-il arinjirikanam. Ithu life-saving aanu!

**Topic: Emergency Numbers (Die Notrufnummern)**
Germany-yil moonu important numbers und. Actually, main randu numbers aanu.
- **112**: "Rettungsdienst & Feuerwehr" (Ambulance and Fire Brigade). Ithu Europe-wide common number aanu. Accident or medical emergency undekil eppozhum call 112!
- **110**: "Polizei" (Police). Criminal activity or police intervention undekil call 110. (Remember: Kerala-yil 100 pole polizei 110).

Simple trick: 110 for police, 112 for fire/ambulance (The rescue Duo!).

**Topic: Calling for Help (Hilfe rufen!)**
First thing: "Ich brauche Hilfe!" (I need help! / Enikku sahayam venam!).
"Rufen Sie einen Krankenwagen!" (Call an ambulance!). 
"Rufen Sie die Polizei!" (Call the police!).

If you see an accident: "Es gab einen Unfall!" (There was an accident!).

**Topic: Describing the Emergency (The 5 W-Questions)**
Ambulance dispatcher phone-il chodhikkum "Wo sind Sie?" (Where are you?).
- "Was ist passiert?" (What happened?)
- "Wie viele Verletzte?" (How many injured?)
- "Welche Verletzungen?" (What kind of injuries?)
- "Warten auf Rückfragen" (Wait for follow-up questions — don't hang up until they say!).

Kerala-yil nammal "Ee street-il aana" ennu parayunna polle correct block and street name parayuka. House number and city-yum specify cheyyaam. 

**Topic: Location (Wo ist es passiert?)**
- "Ich bin in der [Street Name], Hausnummer [Number]." 
- "In [City Name]."

Important detail: "Google Maps" location share cheyyaan option illenkil location address-um correct aayittu parayuka. 

**Topic: Common Emergency Words**
- "der Notfall" (emergency)
- "der Krankenwagen" (ambulance)
- "der Unfall" (accident)
- "die Verletzung" (injury)
- "das Blut" (blood)
- "das Feuer" (fire / thee)

**Topic: Notaufnahme (ER / Intensive Care)**
If you go to the hospital directly: "Notaufnahme" (Emergency room). 
"Ich brauche sofort Hilfe!" (I need help immediately!).
"Mein Kind hat Atemnot." (My child has breathing difficulty). 

Caution: Minor issues (like head pain or minor fever) — don't use Notaufnahme! Use "Bereitschaftsdienst" (on-call doctor system) or wait for a regular Praxis. Notaufnahme is only for life-threatening situations!

**Closing:**
Emergency numbers, calling phrases, and location details — ee moonu karyangal oru diary-yil ezhuthi vekkuka. Ippo ningal safe aayi work cheyyu.
