## Script

**Narration (Malayalam/Manglish):**
Hello machane! Innu nammalkku "Eating Out" (bahir poyi bhakshanam kazhikkunnathe) patti samsarikkaam. Kerala-yil nammude oru restaurant culture polle alla Germany-yil. Avide kure social rules and manners und.

**Topic: Reservations (Reservierung)**
Aadyatheth "Reservierung" (Reservation). Germany-yile popular restaurants-il chennal table orippozhum empty aavilla. Direct aayittu poyal seat kittaan bhayankara budhimutt aayirikkum.
Phone-ilo website-ilo reservieren cheyyunnathu nallathaanu. "Hi, ich möchte einen Tisch für zwei Personen reservieren." (I would like to reserve a table for two).

**Topic: Water (Wasser) Trap**
Super important info: KERALA-YILE POLLE RESTAURANT-IL FREE WATER KITTILLA! 
Ningal water chodichal, avaru bottle water kondu varum (often Sparkling/Gas water). Oralkku oru bottle mathram aayirikkum charge cheyyunnath (around 4-5 Euro - almost 400+ Rupees!). 
Waitress chodikkum "Still oder Sprudel?" (Still water or sparkling water?). If you want normal tap water, ask "Leitungswasser". Some restaurants will give it free, but many charge small amount. Mostly drinks-nu vendi cash nallapole pokum.

**Topic: Bread and Pretzels**
Chila samayangalil table-il bread or pretzels undaayirikkum. Ithu Kerala-yil free pappadam polle alla! Ningal athu kashichal extra add aavum. "Brotkorb" (bread basket)-num charge undaavam. Check the menu or ask.

**Topic: Splitting the Bill (Getrennt zahlen)**
Group aayittu poyal, common bill varum. Waiter-ode "Zusammen oder getrennt?" (Together or separate?) ennu chodhikkum. 
German logic "Split everything" (Dutch pay) aanu. "Getrennt, bitte" (Separate, please) ennu paranjaal individual charges Waiter calculate cheyyum. No confusion later!

**Topic: Tipping (Trinkgeld)**
Germany-yil tipping mandatory alla, but expect cheyyum. Usually 5-10% of the total amount "Trinkgeld" (Tip) aayi kodukkum.
If your bill is 18.50 Euro, say "Machen Sie 20 Euro" (Make it 20 Euro). Ennu vachaal bakki amount tip aayi waiter edukkatte ennaanu artham. No need to leave cash on the table! Waiter card reader-ilo cash-ilo ethra mathram pay cheyyanam ennu paranjal mathi.

**Topic: Manners (Knigge)**
Table manners valare crucial aanu. Spoon and fork rules und. "Guten Appetit!" ennathe oru basic wishing phrase aanu meal thudangunna munnil. Food kazhichu kazhinjal fork and knife 4 o'clock position-il parallal aayi vekkunnatha "I'm done" enna indication.

**Closing:**
Reservation, water trap, splitting the bill, and tipping — ee naalu karyangal manasilakkiyaal German dining-il ningal smooth aayirikkum! 

Adutha module-il nammal "Health and Body" (Gezundheit) patti padikkaam. 

Until then, Guten Appetit and Tschüss!
