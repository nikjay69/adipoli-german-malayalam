## Script

**Narration (Malayalam/Manglish):**
Namskaram machane! Aadyathe lesson-il nammal rental agreement (Mietvertrag) padichu. Ini, Germany-yil "House Rules" (Hausordnung) valare strict aanu. Kerala-yile polle noisy or careless aakan pattilla. "Mülltrennung" (Trash separation) and "Ruhezeiten" (Quiet hours) patti nammalkku detail aayittu padikkaam.

**Topic: Quiet Hours (Ruhezeiten)**
Germany-yile oru holy thing aanu "Ruhezeit". Common house rules-ul kure points und:
- **Nachtruhe** (Night quiet): 22:00 up to 06:00 or 07:00. Pattu loud aayittu kettilla, noisy parties okke 10 PM kazhinjal prohibited aanu. Neighbors complaint cheyyaam!
- **Sonntagsruhe** (Sunday quiet): 24 hours Sunday! Sunday or public holidays-il drill machine or loud vacuum cleaner okke use cheyyunnathu avoids cheyyaam. (Keep the peace, machane!).
- **Mittagsruhe** (Noon quiet): Depends on the house, but sometimes 13:00 to 15:00.

"Vermeiden Sie Lärm während der Ruhezeiten." (Avoid noise during quiet hours).

**Topic: Trash Separation (Mülltrennung)**
German logic "Trash separation" (different bins) valare detailed aanu. Kerala-yil nammal mix cheyyunnathu polle cheyyaan pattilla.
- **Biomüll** (Bio-waste / Brown bin): Food waste (pazhamtholi, vegetables, cooked food).
- **Papier** (Paper / Blue bin): Boxes, newspaper, letters. (Flatten the boxes, don't just dump!).
- **Verpackungen / Wertstoff** (Plastic and Metal / Yellow bin): Plastic bottles (rezeptfrei ones), yoghourt cups, metal cans.
- **Restmüll** (Residual waste / Grey or Black bin): Cleaning items, old shoes, stuff that doesn't fit in other 3!
- **Glas** (Glass): Separate bins outside for Green, Brown, and White glass. (Don't put glass in your house bin!).

"Trennen Sie den Müll richtig." (Separate the trash correctly). Fine varaan chance und!

**Topic: Cleaning Common Areas (Kehrwoche / Flurreinigung)**
Sila apartments-ul "Hausmeister" (Janitor) undaavu. But sila small buildings-ul "Kehrwoche" (Cleaning week) system undaavu. Weekly or monthly turn-vazhi staircase/hallway cleaning cheyyanam. Door-ul oru small sign "Kehrwoche" undaavu. Switch clean cheyyunnathu orma vekkuka!

**Topic: Venting the House (Lüften)**
In Germany, energy-efficient apartments are airtight. Athu kondu mould (fumbus) varaan chance und.
- **Stoßlüften** (Flash venting): Opening windows completely for 5-10 minutes twice a day. (Not tilting! Opening!). Athu dampness reduce cheyyaanum fresh air tharanum help cheyyum. This is expected by the landlord!

**Topic: Interaction Drills**
Neighbor: "Guten Tag, Herr Nair! Bitte seien Sie am Abend etwas leiser. Mein Baby schläft ab 20 Uhr."
Nair: "Oh, das tut mir leid. Ich werde leise sein. Danke für den Hinweis!"
Neighbor: "Haben Sie heute Kehrwoche?"
Nair: "Ja, ich fange am Nachmittag an zu putzen. Alles klar!"

Adholoke! Simple polite interaction with neighbors.

**Closing:**
Quiet hours, trash separation, venting, and kehrwoche — ee house rules ippo ningalkku ariyaam. Good neighbor aakaan ready aayikko?

Adutha lesson-il nammal "Moving In" (Einzug & Schlüssel) patti samsarikkaam. See you in the next video! Bye!
