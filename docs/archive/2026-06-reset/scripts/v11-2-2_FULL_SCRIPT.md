## Script

**Narration (Malayalam/Manglish):**
Namskaram machane! Aadyathe lesson-il nammal office culture patti samsarichu. Ini, Germany-yil "University" (Universität / Hochschule) life-ne kurichu samsarikkaam. Kerala-yil ninno Bangalore-yil ninno university-kku poyatheyalla, Germany-yil system common things und. "Studentenleben" (Student Life) engane aano ennu nokkaam.

**Topic: Campus Spaces (Campus-Gelände)**
Aadyatheth "Mensa" (University Cafeteria). Germany-yile student-ukku heart aanu ithu. Very cheap and healthy food kittum. "Ich gehe in die Mensa." (I am going to the cafeteria).
- "die Bibliothek" (the library / Bib): "Ich lerne in der Bibliothek."
- "der Hörsaal" (the lecture hall / Auditorium): "Die Vorlesung ist im Hörsaal 101."
- "das Prüfungsamt" (the examination office): Results-um registration-um okke ithu vazhiyaanu.

**Topic: Academic Terms**
- "die Vorlesung" (the lecture): Profesor parayunnath nammal note cheyyunnu. (Mostly optional, but check your course rules!).
- "das Seminar" (the seminar): Kurachu koodi interactive sessions aanu. Presenting papers and discussing okke undaayirikkum.
- "die Hausarbeit" (the term paper): Semester end-il ezhuthunna valiya assignment.
- "die Klausur" (the exam / written exam): Ithaanu main thrill! "Ich schreibe morgen eine Klausur." (I am writing an exam tomorrow).

**Topic: Enrollment and Social Life**
- "die Immatrikulation" (enrollment / matriculation): Oru semester thudangunna munnil admission and fee payment status.
- "das Semesterticket" (the semester ticket): Germany-yile ella student-ukkum ithu king aanu! Almost free common transport across the city or even the state (depends on your uni).
- "das Studentenwohnheim" (the student dorm): University-yute accommodation rooms. Rent kuravaayirikkum, so apply early! "Ich wohne im Studentenwohnheim."

**Topic: Study Groups (Lerngruppen)**
"Lerngruppe" — Ithu Germany-yil valare popular aanu. Malayali groups polle thanne students together and collaborate. Together solutions find cheyyunnathu German style aanu. Join or start a "Lerngruppe"!

**Topic: Independent Learning**
Kerala-yil nammal mostly textbooks and teachers follow cheyyunnu. Germany-yil "Selbststudium" (self-study) valare important aanu. Teachers direction tharum, but detailed aayittu nammal thanne search and learn cheyyanam. Peer learning and research aanu avide key.

**Topic: Useful Student Phrases**
- "Haben wir heute eine Vorlesung?" (Do we have a lecture today?)
- "Wo ist die Mensa?" (Where is the cafeteria?)
- "Ich muss für die Prüfung lernen." (I must study for the exam.)

**Closing:**
Mensa, Bib, Hörsaal and exams — university life-ile ee core words ippo ningalkku ariyaam. Germany-yile student days follow through and enjoy your studies!
