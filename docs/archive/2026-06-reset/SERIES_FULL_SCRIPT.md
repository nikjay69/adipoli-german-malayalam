## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Character Identity — Kuttan
- young Malayali man, early 20s
- warm expressive face, medium-brown skin
- black hair, clean and simple, no beard, no glasses
- casual white cotton shirt, jeans
- small black backpack (departure arc only)
- emotional range across the series: curious → nervous → frustrated → determined → quietly proud → resolved

### Animation Rules
- Subtle natural motion only
- Small breathing, tiny head adjustments
- No complex choreography
- No camera jumps
- No face morphing

### Phone / Notebook / Whiteboard Rules
- Phone content must be meaningful story content, not blank glow
- Notebook handwriting can be visible in-frame as German text (this is the only allowed in-frame text)
- Whiteboards, exam papers, signs: NO readable text — keep them visually suggestive only

### Generation Order (per video)
1. Generate all stills for the video
2. Review stills for character continuity
3. Generate all video clips for that video
4. Combine into rough cut
5. Review
6. Only then move to next video
