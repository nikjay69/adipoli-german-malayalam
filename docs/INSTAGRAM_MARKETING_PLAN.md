## Post Formats & What Works on Instagram (2026)

### Tier 1: CAROUSELS (highest saves & shares — 40% of posts)
Instagram's algorithm rewards saves. Carousels = save machines.

**Why:** People save "useful" carousels to study later. Each save signals the algorithm to push wider. A carousel with 10 German words gets saved 5-10x more than a single image.

**Types:**
- "10 German words you'll use every day" (vocab)
- "5 mistakes every beginner makes" (grammar)
- "How to introduce yourself in German (step by step)" (tutorial)
- "Kerala food in German" (culture + vocab)
- "Goethe A1 exam: what to expect" (exam prep)

### Tier 2: REELS (highest reach — 30% of posts)
Reels are how you get discovered by non-followers.

**Why:** Instagram pushes Reels to the Explore page. A 15-second Reel can reach 10-50x your follower count. This is your growth engine.

**Types:**
- "Repeat after me: 5 German greetings" (speaking drill)
- "POV: You try to order food in Germany" (skit)
- "German word of the day" (quick teach)
- "Things that shock Malayalees in Germany" (relatable culture)
- "Malayalam to German: words that sound similar" (mind-blown hooks)
- Trending audio + German learning twist

### Tier 3: SINGLE IMAGE POSTS (brand building — 15% of posts)
Clean, branded, shareable cards.

**Types:**
- "Word of the Day" vocab card
- Motivational quote (German + translation)
- Quick grammar rule (one card, one rule)
- "This or That" (der or das?)

### Tier 4: STORIES (engagement — daily)
Stories don't grow followers but they retain them.

**Types:**
- Daily quiz: "What does ___ mean?" (poll sticker)
- "Guess the German word" (quiz sticker)
- Behind-the-scenes: building the app
- "Ask me anything about German/Germany"
- Countdown to launches/features
- Share follower DMs/testimonials (with permission)

### Script: `generate-instagram.ts`

**What it does:**
1. Accepts a week number or date range
2. Auto-selects content based on the content calendar:
   - Mon: Word of the Day
   - Tue: Vocab Carousel (10 slides)
   - Wed: Meme / Relatable
   - Thu: Grammar Tip Carousel
   - Fri: Reel Script (text only — human records)
   - Sat: Culture / Life post
   - Sun: Exam Tip / Motivation
3. Pulls German content from verified module data (zero error risk)
4. Calls Gemini 2.5 Flash for captions, hooks, meme text, culture facts
5. Renders all images using Puppeteer branded templates
6. Outputs everything to a folder ready for review + scheduling

**Estimated cost per month:**
- ~120 Gemini API calls × ~500 tokens each = ~60K tokens/month
- Gemini 2.5 Flash: ~$0.05/month (essentially free)
- Image rendering: $0 (local Puppeteer)
- **Total: under $0.10/month for 30 posts**

## Growth Strategy — How to Get Followers

- Every post must be SAVEABLE or SHAREABLE — ask yourself "would I send this to a friend?"

**The Hashtag Strategy:**
- Use 15-20 hashtags per post (still effective in 2026 for discovery)
- Mix of sizes:
  - Big (1M+): #LearnGerman, #German, #LanguageLearning, #StudyAbroad
  - Medium (100K-1M): #GermanA1, #GermanLanguage, #StudyInGermany, #GermanVocabulary

## Error Prevention Protocol (CRITICAL)

For a language teaching account, **one wrong article = credibility destroyed.**

### Rules:
1. **ALL German in posts must come from the verified codebase** (758 vocab items, grammar rules, exercises)
2. **Gemini generates ONLY Manglish captions and hooks** — never German content
3. **Every post goes through this checklist before publishing:**
   - [ ] German spelling correct?
   - [ ] Article correct? (der/die/das)
   - [ ] Case correct? (Nom/Akk/Dat)
   - [ ] Example sentence grammatically correct?
   - [ ] Pronunciation guide accurate?
4. **Build a review step into the automation pipeline** — output goes to a review folder, not directly to posting
5. **When in doubt, omit** — better to teach less than to teach wrong
