| Section | Duration | Points | Weight |

## Cross-Section Gap Summary

### Fully Covered (✅ No critical gaps)
- Lesen — exercises exist, content is strong, mainly needs timed mode
- Schreiben Teil 2 — /practice/write with 20 prompts and AI feedback
- Sprechen Teil 1 — /practice/intro with 6 topics and timer

### Partially Covered (⚠️ Addressable gaps)
- Schreiben Teil 1 — form-filling drills needed as production exercises
- Sprechen Teil 2 — needs random topic-card generator
- Sprechen Teil 3 — needs situation-card request drills
- Hören all sections — audio generation is the single biggest remaining gap

### Critical Gaps (🔴 Blocks exam readiness)
1. **Real listening audio** — Without generated audio, Hören section cannot be honestly prepared
2. **Production exercises** — Course is still >90% recognition; exam is ~50% production
3. **Form-filling drills** — Schreiben Teil 1 needs practice forms, not just vocabulary
4. **Dictation exercises** — Bridge between Hören and Schreiben (listen → write)
