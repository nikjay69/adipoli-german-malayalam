## Script

**Narration (Malayalam/Manglish):**
Namskaram machane! Innu nammalkku "Enikku vayya" ennu engane German-il complete sentences parayaam ennu padikkaam. Ithu common ailments (disease) are.

"Tut weh" padicha pole thaan "Ich habe ..." (I have ...) structure. Ithu kure important words ormipichu vekkuka.

**Topic: Common Ailments (Common Pains)**
Germany-yil ningalkku ethra common sick scenarios und: "Kopfschmerzen", "Fieber", "Husten", "Schnupfen".

- "Ich habe Kopfschmerzen." (I have a headache)
- "Ich habe Bauchschmerzen." (I have a stomachache)
- "Ich habe Fieber." (I have a fever / Enikku pani undu)

**Topic: Compound Nouns (Stacking Lego Blocks)**
Nammal oru amazing thing kure thavana samsarichirunnallo — German-il words join cheyyam.
[Body Part] + "Schmerzen" (pain) = [Symptom].

- "Kopf" (head) + "Schmerzen" = "Kopfschmerzen"
- "Bauch" (stomach) + "Schmerzen" = "Bauchschmerzen"
- "Zahn" (tooth) + "Schmerzen" = "Zahnschmerzen" (toothache)
- "Hals" (throat) + "Schmerzen" = "Halsschmerzen" (sore throat)

Ithu simple formula aanu! Just body part + "schmerzen" = pain description.

**Topic: Fever, Cough, Runny Nose**
Pain allaatheyulla mattu symptoms:
- "Husten" (cough): "Ich habe Husten." 
- "Schnupfen" (runny nose / cold): "Ich habe Schnupfen."
- "Grippe" (flu): "Ich habe die Grippe."

**Topic: The Dativ Feelings (Mir is...)**
Ithu super important aanu machane. Feelings-nu nammal "Mir ist ..." ennathe upayogikkuka (Dativ). 

- "Mir ist schlecht." (I feel nauseous / unhealthy)
- "Mir ist kalt." (I feel cold)
- "Mir ist warm." (I feel warm)

ORMA VEKKUKA: "Mir ist schlecht" ennu parayanam. "Ich bin schlecht" ennu paranjaal athinte artham "Njan oru bad person aanu" ennaanu! So don't be a villain, use "Mir"!

**Closing:**
Symptoms or ailments parayaan "Ich habe ..." or "Mir ist ..." — ee things manasilakkiyaal simple aayirikkum. Doctor-ude aduthu ithu bhayankara helpful aayirikkum.

Adutha lesson-il nammal "At the Doctor" (Beim Arzt) patti samsarikkaam. See you in the next video! Bye!
