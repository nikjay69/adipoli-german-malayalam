## Section 2: The static "Wo?" — Location vs. Motion (0:50–2:30)

### Visual: [HIGHLIGHT: "WO?" (Where?) + "Sein" (to be) = DATIVE. Icon: A still camera (static location).]
### Narration: [ENERGY: steady, instructional]
"In German, when something is STAYING STILL in a place, the question is **'Wo?'** (Where?).

[The static rule]
When you answer 'Wo?', the prepositions like 'in', 'auf', or 'neben' use the **DATIVE** case.

[Dative review]
- **der** → **dem**
- **das** → **dem**
- **die** → **der**

🔊 **'Das Buch ist auf dem Tisch.'**
(The book is ON the table — Tisch is 'der' so it becomes 'dem').

*Static location-il 'dem' and 'der' follow cheyyanam. Ithu nannaayi orkkanam!*

⏸️ **Try: In the room. (das Zimmer)**
### Practice Pause: ⏸️ (2 seconds)
**im Zimmer.** ✅ *(in + dem = im!)*"

---

## Section 3: The Big 4 pairs — Location Drills (2:30–4:30)

### Visual: [ICONS: 1. auf (on) / unter (under) | 2. vor (in front of) / hinter (behind). Arrows illustrating the positions.]
### Narration: [ENERGY: building, logical]
"Let's drill the first four.

[Group 1: Top & Bottom]
1. 🔊 **auf dem Tisch** (on the table).
2. 🔊 **unter dem Tisch** (under the table).

[Group 2: Front & Back]
3. 🔊 **vor dem Schrank** (in front of the wardrobe).
4. 🔊 **hinter dem Schrank** (behind the wardrobe).

*Opposites-aayi padichal nalla-thaanu alle? 'Auf' vs 'Unter', 'Vor' vs 'Hinter'.*

⏸️ **Try: Under the bed. (das Bett)**
### Practice Pause: ⏸️ (2 seconds)
**unter dem Bett.** ✅"

---

## Section 4: Next to & Between — neben & zwischen (4:30–6:30)

### Visual: [ICONS: 1. neben (next to) | 2. zwischen (between A and B). Animation showing the object's position.]
### Narration: [ENERGY: building, logical]
"Two more essentials for the layout.

[Words appear]
- 🔊 **neben dem Bett** — *Next to the bed.*
- 🔊 **zwischen dem Tisch und dem Sofa** — *Between the table and the sofa.*

*Zwischen choyikkumpol 'und' koode add cheyyunna-thaanu correct structure!*

⏸️ **Try: Next to the lamp. (die Lampe)**
### Practice Pause: ⏸️ (3 seconds)
**neben der Lampe.** ✅ *(die → der!)*"

## Section 5: Malayalam Logic — "Near" and "On" (6:30–8:00)

### Visual: [TABLE: Malayalam 'Mesa mukhil' vs German 'auf dem Tisch'. 'Kattilinte aduthu' vs 'neben dem Bett'.]
### Narration: [ENERGY: cultural, structural]
"In Malayalam, we use suffixes like '-il' or '-inte aduthu' (next to).

German uses preposition words *before* the noun, and then 'dances' with the article.
- 'In the kitchen' → **'in der Küche'** (Küche is die).
- 'Next to the fridge' → **'neben dem Kühlschrank'** (Kühlschrank is der).

*German words-inte position important aanu. Preposition first-il vename, dative article center-il vename.*

[EXAM-TIP] The examiner will ask: 'Wo ist der Fernseher?' (Where is the TV?). Answer with 'neben dem...' or 'auf dem...' to show you know your dative articles!"
