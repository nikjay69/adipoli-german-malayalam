## Script

**Narration (Malayalam/Manglish):**
Namskaram machane! Aadyathe lesson-il nammal social openers padichu. Ini, "Keeping the Conversation Flowing" (Communication bridges) and "Responses" (Ethirthu samsaraam) detail aayittu German-il samsarikkaam enn nokkaam. Ithu networking-il valare essential aanu.

**Topic: Active Listening (Echoing and Confirming)**
- "Ach so!" (I see! / Ah athre aarno!).
- "Echt?" (Really? / Sathyam aarno!).
- "Das ist interessant!" (That's interesting! / Ithu kure nallathaanu!).
- "Genau!" (Exactly! / Athu thanne!).
- "Wunderbar!" (Wonderful! / Super!).

Ithu eppozhum conversational interest show cheyyaan help cheyyum.

**Topic: Asking Back (Und du? / Und Sie?)**
If someone asks you something, answer and always ask back:
- "Ich wohne in Berlin. Und du?" (I live in Berlin. And you?).
- "Ich bin Informatiker. Und Sie?" (I'm an IT guy. And you?).
- "Ich koche gern. Was machen Sie in Ihrer Freizeit?" (I like to cook. What do you do in your free time?).

"Und du?" / "Und Sie?" ennathe word orma vekkuka. Reverse questioning!

**Topic: Personal Status (Safe and Unsafe Topics)**
Safe:
- **Travel** (Reisen): "Wohin reisen Sie gern?" (Where do you like to travel?).
- **Sports** (Sport): "Mögen Sie Fußball?"
- **Food** (Essen): "Was essen Sie gern?"

Unsafe (Wait until you know them better!):
- **Money** (Geld): "Wie viel verdienen Sie?" (No!).
- **Politics** (Politik): "Welche Partei mögen Sie?" (Careful!).
- **Religion** (Religion): (Avoid initially!).

**Topic: Keeping Interested (More follow up questions)**
- "Wie lange leben Sie schon hier?" (How long have you been living here?).
- "Gefällt es Ihnen in Berlin?" (Do you like it in Berlin?).
- "Was machen Sie beruflich?" (What do you do for work?).

**Topic: Closing the Conversation (Polite Leave Taking)**
- "Es hat mich gefreut, Sie kennenzulernen." (It was a pleasure to meet you).
- "Wir sehen uns bald!" (We see each other soon!).
- "Schönen Abend noch!" (Have a nice evening / Pinne kaanam).

**Topic: Interaction Drills (Practical Simulation)**
"Ich komme aus Indien, aus Kerala." (Aadyathe statement).
"Oh, Indien! Das ist interessant! Ich war letztes Jahr in Goa." (Active listening part).
"Echt? Wie hat es Ihnen gefallen?" (Asking back part).
"Es war wunderbar! Das Essen war sehr lecker." (Response).
"Ja, das glaube ich gern! Und Sie? Woher kommen Sie?" (Asking back again).
"Ich bin aus Stuttgart." (Final answer).

Adholoke! Simple polite communication.

**Closing:**
Active listening, asking back, and safe topics — ee moonu karyangal ippo ningalkku ariyaam. Conversation flow set aayo?
