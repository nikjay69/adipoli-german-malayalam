## Script

**Narration (Malayalam/Manglish):**
Namskaram machane! Aadyathe lesson-il nammal skills okke padichu. Ini, "Certificates and Degrees" (Qualifikationen) patti engane detail aayittu German-il parayaam enn nokkaam. Germany-yil credentials valare important aanu. Almost ellaadathum documents and certificates chodhikkum.

**Topic: Certificates (das Zeugnis)**
"das Zeugnis" (the certificate / paper). Ithu schooling results muthal workplace review vare ellam aanu. 
- "Ich habe ein Universitätszeugnis." (I have a university certificate)
- "Ich habe gute Noten." (I have good grades)
- "Ich habe meine Zeugnisse im Anhang." (I have my certificates in the attachment).

**Topic: Degrees (der Abschluss)**
"der Abschluss" (the degree / qualification completion).
- "Ich habe einen Bachelor-Abschluss." (I have a Bachelor's degree / Njan Bachelor degree edutha)
- "Ich habe einen Master-Abschluss in BWL." (I have a Master's degree in Business Administration)
- "Ich habe im Jahr 2021 meinen Abschluss gemacht." (I completed my degree in 2021)

**Topic: Language Proficiency (das Sprachzertifikat)**
Germany-yile language certificates (IELTS/OET pole) valare standard aayirikkum. Use "das Sprachzertifikat" or "das Zertifikat".
- "Ich habe das A1-Zertifikat von Goethe-Institut." (I have A1 certificate from Goethe)
- "Ich bewerbe mich mit dem B1-Zertifikat." (I am applying with B1 certificate)
- "Ich besuche zurzeit einen Deutschkurs." (I am currently attending a German course)

Important Tip: A1/A2 (Beginner), B1/B2 (Intermediate), C1/C2 (Advanced). Mention your level clearly and truthfully!

**Topic: Professional Driving (der Führerschein)**
"der Führerschein" (the driving license). Germany-yile manual or automatic preference and other transport roles-nu ithu secondary qualification aayi chodhikkum.
- "Ich habe einen Führerschein der Klasse B." (I have a class B driving license).
- "Ich habe meinen Führerschein in Indien gemacht." (I made my driving license in India).

Note: Indian driving license Germany-yil first 6 months work cheyyum, but later conversion or fresh test must aanu. Useful career tip!

**Topic: Further Education (die Weiterbildung)**
"die Weiterbildung" (extra training / continuing education). Semester-ukku purathekku nammal cheyyuna extra courses okke "Weiterbildung" aanu.
- "Ich mache eine Weiterbildung im Bereich Projektmanagement." (I am doing training in project management).
- "Ich habe an einem Online-Kurs teilgenommen." (I participated in an online course).

**Topic: Mastering a Skill (beherrschen)**
"beherrschen" (to master / command). Ithu "können"-ne kaalum kooduthal intensity ulla word aanu. 
- "Ich beherrsche Python und Java fließend." (I master Python and Java fluently).
- "Ich beherrsche SAP-Software."

**Closing:**
Zeugnis, Abschluss, and Sprachzertifikat — qualifications describe cheyyaan ippo ningalkku ariyaam. Correct documents undekil Germany-yil ningal safe aanu.

Adutha lesson-il nammal "Job Interview Basics" (Grundlagen des Vorstellungsgesprächs) patti padikkaam. Prepare cheyyan ready aayikko? See you then! Bye!
