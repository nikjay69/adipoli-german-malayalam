## Section 2: The Greeting — "Kann ich Ihnen helfen?" (0:50–2:30)

### Visual: [ICONS: Sales assistant (formal). Caption: "Kann ich Ihnen helfen?" (Can I help you?). Speech bubbles appearing.]
### Narration: [ENERGY: steady, instructional]
"As soon as you walk in, a sales assistant will greet you.

[The Greeting]
🔊 **'Guten Tag! Kann ich Ihnen helfen?'**
(Can I help you? — Note the formal 'Ihnen').

[Your 2 Responses]
1. 🔊 **'Nein danke, ich schaue nur.'** — *No thanks, I'm just looking.*
2. 🔊 **'Ja bitte, ich suche...'** — *Yes please, I'm looking for...*

*Sales assistant choyikkumpol ninnalkku 'ich schaue nur' (just looking) ennu parayaam. It's safe!*

⏸️ **Try: I'm just looking.**
### Practice Pause: ⏸️ (2 seconds)
**Ich schaue nur.** ✅"

---

## Section 3: The Search — "Ich suche..." (2:30–4:30)

### Visual: [HIGHLIGHT: "Ich suche..." + [Noun]. Examples: Ich suche ein Hemd. Ich suche eine Jacke.]
### Narration: [ENERGY: building, logical]
"If you need something specific, use 🔊 **'Ich suche...'** (I'm looking for).

[Formula]
- **'Ich suche ein Hemd.'** (I need a shirt).
- **'Ich suche eine Hose.'** (I need trousers).
- **'Ich suche einen Pullover.'** (I need a sweater — note the '-en' for male accusative!).

*Suchen-nu 'Ich' form use cheyyumpol 'suche' ennu thanne parayanam. Prefix or endings are simple!*

⏸️ **Say: I'm looking for a (female) jacket.**
### Practice Pause: ⏸️ (2 seconds)
**Ich suche eine Jacke.** ✅"

---

## Section 4: The Fitting Check — Size & Try-On (4:30–6:30)

### Visual: [HIGHLIGHT: 1. "Haben Sie das in Größe...?" | 2. "Kann ich das anprobieren?". Icon: Fitting room (Umkleidekabine).]
### Narration: [ENERGY: building, logical]
"You found a shirt you like, but you need another size.

[Phrases]
- 🔊 **'Haben Sie das in Größe M?'** (Do you have this in size M?)
- 🔊 **'Haben Sie das in Blau?'** (Do you have this in blue?)

Now, you want to try it on.
- 🔊 **'Kann ich das anprobieren?'** (Can I try this on?)
- 🔊 **'Wo ist die Umkleidekabine?'** (Where is the fitting room?)

*Anprobieren (to try on) — word order anusharichu 'Kann ich...' question-il end-il varum.*

⏸️ **Ask: Where is the fitting room?**
### Practice Pause: ⏸️ (2 seconds)
**Wo ist die Umkleidekabine?** ✅"

---

## Section 5: Malayalam Comparison — Asking politely (6:30–8:00)

### Visual: [TABLE: Malayalam 'Size undey?' vs German 'Haben Sie das in Größe...?'.]
### Narration: [ENERGY: cultural, structural]
"In Kerala, we're very direct — 'Size undey?' or 'Check cheyyamo?'.

In German, we use **'Haben Sie'** (Have you) and **'Kann ich'** (Can I). It's more about permission and politeness. Using these phrases makes the sales staff much more helpful!

*Nammude formal tone German-il 'Sie' form-il varunnatha-thaanu exam-il credit kittan help cheyyuka!*

[EXAM-TIP] The examiner will listen for **'Größe'** (size) and **'anprobieren'**. If you include these, you're at A1-Expert level!"

---

## Section 6: The Buy — "Ich nehme es" (8:00–10:00)

### Visual: [HIGHLIGHT: 1. "Ich nehme es." | 2. "Was kostet das?". Icon: Cash register (Kasse).]
### Narration: [ENERGY: attentive, instructional]
"Final step — decision and payment.

[Phrases]
- 🔊 **'Das passt gut! Ich nehme es.'** — *It fits well! I'll take it.*
- 🔊 **'Wie viel kostet das?'** — *How much does it cost?*
- 🔊 **'Wo ist die Kasse?'** — *Where is the checkout?*

*Kasse... Cash register location choyikkan easy aanu alle? Pay cheyyan ippol nee ready aanu!*

⏸️ **Say: I'll take it!**
### Practice Pause: ⏸️ (2 seconds)
**Ich nehme es.** ✅"

---

## Section 7: Speed Challenge — Shopaholic Drill (10:00–12:00)

### Visual: [FLASHCARDS: Scenario (Want shirt, ask size, try on). Student says the full phrase.]
### Narration: [ENERGY: high, rapid-fire]
"Rapid fire. What do you say?

⚡ You're entering a shop? → **'Ich schaue nur!'** (Just looking).
⚡ You want a sweater? → **'Ich suche einen Pullover!'**
⚡ You want size L? → **'Haben Sie das in Größe L?'**
⚡ You want to try it on? → **'Kann ich das anprobieren?'**
⚡ You're done? → **'Das ist alles, danke!'**

Adipoli! You're the shopping king/queen of the class!."
