>
> *"Office vocabulary easy aanu — most words look similar to English!"*

> 🔊 "Ich habe eine Frage." — I have a question.

**Narrator:**
> "Now the university. die Uni — everyone says Uni, not the full Universität."
>
> "die Vorlesung — a lecture. der Hörsaal — the lecture HALL. Hör- means 'to hear' and Saal means hall. It's literally a 'hearing hall'!"
>
> "die Mensa — the university canteen. Not a restaurant — much cheaper! Very important for students."
>
> "die Bibliothek — the library. die Prüfung — the exam. der Stundenplan — your timetable."
>
> ⏸️ **[Quiz: What's the German word for lecture? → die Vorlesung!]**

**[PHRASES on screen]**

### Scene 7: Email at Work / Uni (9:00–10:30)
**[SLIDE: Short email template]**

**Narrator:**
> "One more real-world skill: sending a short email in German."
>
> **Template:**
> ```
> Betreff: Frage zum Stundenplan
>
> Sehr geehrte Frau Schmidt,
>
> ich bin neu an der Universität.
> Können Sie mir bitte den Stundenplan schicken?
>
> Vielen Dank!
> Mit freundlichen Grüßen
> Arun Kumar
> ```
>
> "Betreff = Subject line. 'Sehr geehrte Frau...' = Dear Mrs... (formal). 'Mit freundlichen Grüßen' = Kind regards."
>
> "For your boss or professor, ALWAYS use 'Sehr geehrte/r' and 'Sie'. Never 'du'!"
>
> ⏸️ **[Try writing a similar email asking about a meeting time]**
>
> *"German email etiquette strict aanu — but this template will save you every time!"*
