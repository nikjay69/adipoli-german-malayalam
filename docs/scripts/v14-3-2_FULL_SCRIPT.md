## Script

**Narration (Malayalam/Manglish):**
Machane! Aadyathe lesson-il nammal city-kku ullil ulla transport-ne kurichu samsarichu. Ini, oru city-yil ninnum vere oru city-lekku (long distance) travel cheyyan Germany-yil enthanu pokunnathennu nokkaam. "Intercity" travel-inte king "Deutsche Bahn" (DB) aanu. 

Germany-yil trains moonu main types-ukkalil aanu varunnath: "ICE", "IC", and "Regional-Züge".

**Topic: ICE (Intercity-Express)**
"ICE" ennu parayunnathu high-speed bullet train systems aanu. Ithinte top speed around 300+ km/h aanu. Super fast!
Berlin-nil ninnum Munich-lekku 4-5 hours-il reach aavaam. Ticket-inte rate itihiri kooduthalaaayirikkum. Adharu seat reservation mandatory illa, but recommended aanu. Comfortable aayirikkum — like Rajdhani or Shatabdi in India, but way faster and sleek!

**Topic: IC / EC (Intercity / Eurocity)**
"IC" (Intercity) or "EC" (Eurocity) ICE-ne kaalum itihiri kooduthal stops-ukkal ulla trains aanu. But high speed aanu. "EC" ennu parayunnathu other European countries (Austria, Switzerland, etc.)-lekku pokunna route-ukkal aanu. 

**Topic: Regional-Züge (RB / RE)**
Regional Trains — "RB" (Regionalbahn) and "RE" (Regional-Express). Ithu city-kku purathe ulla small towns-ukkal-lekku travel cheyyunna system. 
RE trains itihiri kooduthal fast and important stops matrame undaavu. RB stops almost ella stations-ilum undaayirikkum. Simple standard tickets (like Deutschland-Ticket or Bayern-Ticket) Regional-Züge-il effective aanu. Usually unlimited travel for one day or one month configuration-il undayirikkum.

Important Caution: Regional-ticket (monthly passes) eduthaal athu ICE or IC-il work cheyyilla. ICE-il ticket eduthaale athil keraan pattu. Confusion varaathe nokkuka!

**Topic: Delay (Verspätung)**
Train delay-ine kurichu Germany-yil kure jokes und. DB logic: 5 minutes delay ennu parayunnathu delay aayi avaru karuthilla! So, 10-15 minutes delay sometimes occur aavaam. Delay undekil, display boards (Gleis-Anzeige) nokkuka. "Gleiswechsel" (platform change) undekil monitor-il ezhuthi varum. 

**Topic: Bahnhöfe (Train Stations)**
Central stations-ne "Hauptbahnhof" (Hbf) ennu parayum. Munich Hbf, Berlin Hbf okke. Germany-yile ella valiya city-yilum Hbf-inu central connection und. 

**Closing:**
Long distance trains and regional trains-ne patti ippo basic idea kittiyo? 
Germany-yile train journey-ukkal bhayankara scenic aanu, so enjoy your travel! 

Adutha lesson-il nammal "Shopping in Germany" ennu nokkaam. See you in the next one! Bye!
