## Section 2: Cheat Code 1 — The 'Woman' Endings (die) (0:50–4:00)

### Visual: [LIST: The 'die' endings. -ung | -heit | -keit | -schaft | -tion | -ie. Color: Red.]
### Narration: [ENERGY: steady, instructional]
"These are the most reliable rules in German. If a word ends in these letters, it's almost ALWAYS **'die'**.

[Highlights appear]
- 🔊 **-ung** — *die Wohnung (the apartment), die Zeitung (the newspaper).*
- 🔊 **-heit / -keit** — *die Freiheit (freedom), die Möglichkeit (possibility).*
- 🔊 **-schaft** — *die Freundschaft (friendship).*
- 🔊 **-tion** — *die Station, die Information.*
- 🔊 **-ie** — *die Familie, die Energie.*

*Check out 'Wohnung' and 'Zeitung' — ivayellam '-ung' ending aanu, so 'die' thanne! Simple aanu, alle?*

⏸️ **Try: Exercise is 'Übung'. What's the article?**
### Practice Pause: ⏸️ (2 seconds)
**die Übung.** ✅"

---

## Section 3: Cheat Code 2 — The 'Man' Categories (der) (4:00–6:30)

### Visual: [LIST: The 'der' categories. Days | Months | Seasons | -er. Color: Blue.]
### Narration: [ENERGY: focused, building]
"Masculine words are also quite logical.

[Logical groups appear]
- **Time Groups:**
  - Days (**der** Montag, **der** Dienstag...)
  - Months (**der** Januar, **der** März...)
  - Seasons (**der** Sommer, **der** Winter...)
- **Ending pattern: -er** (often people/professions)
  - **der** Lehrer (male teacher)
  - **der** Bruder (brother)
  - **der** Computer (computer)

*Note: '-er' ending 70-80% times 'der' aayirikkum. Exceptions exist, but it's a great guess!*

⏸️ **September? (der or die?)**
### Practice Pause: ⏸️ (2 seconds)
**der September.** ✅"

## Section 4: Cheat Code 3 — The 'It' Endings (das) (6:30–8:00)

### Visual: [LIST: The 'das' endings. -ment | -um | -chen | -lein. Color: Green.]
### Narration: [ENERGY: building, logical]
"Finally, the neuter words. Look for these.

[Highlights appear]
- 🔊 **-ment** — *das Dokument, das Instrument.*
- 🔊 **-um** — *das Museum, das Datum.*
- 🔊 **-chen / -lein** — *das Mädchen, das Brötchen. (The 'small' things rule!).*
- 🔊 **Verbs as nouns:**
  - **das Essen** (Food / Eating)
  - **das Lernen** (Learning)

⏸️ **Instrument? (der, die, or das?)**
### Practice Pause: ⏸️ (2 seconds)
**das Instrument.** ✅"
