### Scene 3: The Four Seasons (1:00–2:30)
**[VISUAL: Season wheel with German labels]**

**Narrator:**
> 🔊 "der Frühling" — spring (March–May)
> 🔊 "der Sommer" — summer (June–August)
> 🔊 "der Herbst" — autumn (September–November)
> 🔊 "der Winter" — winter (December–February)
>
> "All seasons are masculine: DER Frühling, DER Sommer, DER Herbst, DER Winter."
>
> "When to use them:"
> 🔊 "im Frühling" — in spring
> 🔊 "im Sommer" — in summer
> 🔊 "im Herbst" — in autumn
> 🔊 "im Winter" — in winter
>
> ⏸️ **[What season is it now? Say it in German!]**
>
> *"im + season = 'in that season'. im Sommer = summer-il. Easy!"*

**Narrator:**
> "The key weather question:"
>
> 🔊 "Wie ist das Wetter?" — How is the weather?
> 🔊 "Wie ist das Wetter heute?" — How is the weather today?
>
> "Answers use 'es ist' or 'es' + verb:"
>
> 🔊 "Es ist sonnig." — It's sunny.
> 🔊 "Es ist kalt." — It's cold.
> 🔊 "Es regnet." — It's raining.
> 🔊 "Es schneit." — It's snowing.
>
> "Temperature:"
> 🔊 "Es sind zwanzig Grad." — It's 20 degrees.
> 🔊 "Es sind minus fünf Grad." — It's minus 5 degrees.
>
> ⏸️ **[Look outside — describe the weather in German!]**
>
> *"Kerala-il 'Es ist heiß' mathi year round! Germany-il daily change aanu — ippo sonnig, pinna regnerisch!"*

### Scene 6: Weather Small Talk Patterns (7:00–8:30)
**[SLIDE: Conversation examples]**

**Narrator:**
> "Germans use weather as small talk. Here's how to join in:"
>
> 🔊 "Schönes Wetter heute!" — Nice weather today!
> 🔊 "Es ist so kalt heute!" — It's so cold today!
> 🔊 "Morgen soll es regnen." — It's supposed to rain tomorrow.
> 🔊 "Im Sommer war es viel wärmer." — In summer it was much warmer.
>
> "Responding:"
> 🔊 "Ja, stimmt!" — Yes, that's right!
> 🔊 "Wirklich? Ich finde es okay." — Really? I think it's okay.
> 🔊 "Ja, ich brauche eine Jacke!" — Yes, I need a jacket!
>
> "These short exchanges build social connection. Use them at work, at the doctor's, in the train — anywhere!"
>
> ⏸️ **[Practice: respond to "Es ist so kalt heute!"]**

### Scene 7: Connecting Weather to Plans (8:30–10:00)
**[SLIDE: Weather + activity sentences]**

**Narrator:**
> "Weather affects plans — learn to express this:"
>
> 🔊 "Wenn es sonnig ist, gehe ich spazieren." — If it's sunny, I go for a walk.
> 🔊 "Wenn es regnet, lese ich ein Buch." — If it rains, I read a book.
> 🔊 "Im Sommer schwimme ich gern." — In summer I like swimming.
> 🔊 "Im Winter trinke ich gern heißen Tee." — In winter I like drinking hot tea.
>
> "The 'wenn' pattern is slightly above A1, but useful to recognize in Hören and Lesen."
>
> "Safe A1 alternative without wenn:"
> 🔊 "Es ist sonnig. Ich gehe spazieren." — Two simple sentences!
>
> *"Mazha peythal pusthakam vaayikkum. Veyil undenkil spazieren gehen. Same concept, German-il!"*
