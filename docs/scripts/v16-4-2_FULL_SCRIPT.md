## Script

**Narration (Malayalam/Manglish):**
Namskaram machane! Aadyathe lesson-il nammal "Perfekt" (e.g., Ich habe gegessen) and "Präteritum" (e.g., Ich aß) padichu. Ini, "Perfekt vs. Präteritum" (When to use which?) detail aayittu German-il samsarikkaam enn nokkaam. Ithu A1+ grammar-il valare essential aayirikkum.

**Topic: Perfekt (Spoken Past)**
"Perfekt" (Spoken past). Ithu nammalkku eppozhum everyday conversation-il use cheyyaam.
- "Ich habe heute gearbeitet." (I have worked today / Njan innu joli cheythu).
- "Wir sind gestern ins Kino gegangen." (We went to the cinema yesterday).

In Kerala-yil nammude normal speech pole thaan, mostly "habe/bin + Partizip II" logic safe aayirikkum. Use Perfekt for everything spoken! (90% case-ul ithaanu usage).

**Topic: Präteritum (Written/Formal Past)**
"Präteritum" (Written past). Ithu books, newspapers, and long letters or stories-ul mathrame common aayittu use cheyyaaru.
- "Gestern war ich müde." (Yesterday I was tired).
- "Ich hatte keine Zeit." (I had no time).

Note: **Exception Verbs** — "sein" (to be) and "haben" (to have) eppozhum Präteritum usage safe aayirikkum even in spoken contexts! "Ich war" is more common than "Ich bin gewesen".

**Topic: The Meaning is the Same!**
In many cases, the meaning of "Ich habe gegessen" and "Ich aß" is exactly the same: **I ate / Njan kazhichu**. 
But logic separation:
- **Spoken** (Conversation): Perfekt.
- **Written** (Books): Präteritum.

**Topic: Modal Verbs (Präteritum Favorite)**
Modal verbs (können, müssen, sollen, wollen, dürfen) eppozhum Präteritum construction logical simple aayirikkum.
- "Ich konnte kommen." (I could come).
- "Ich musste arbeiten." (I had to work).

Perfekt modal verbs: "Ich habe kommen können" (A bit complex! So avoid in speech, use Präteritum instead).

**Topic: Interaction Drills**
Machane, ippo njane oru model comparison introduce cheyyaam.
"Gestern habe ich mit Maria gesprochen." (Spoken / Perfekt).
"Der Reporter sprach mit der Kanzlerin." (Written / Präteritum).
"Ich war gestern krank." (Exception / Präteritum).
"Ich habe keine Suppe gegessen." (Spoken / Perfekt).

Adholoke! Simple natural past flow.

**Closing:**
Perfekt for speaking, Präteritum for writing, and "war/hatte" as exceptions — ee tenses details ippo ningalkku ariyaam. Past tense matching set aayo?
