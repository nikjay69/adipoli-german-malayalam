### Scene 3: Know Your Offices (1:00–3:00)
**[TABLE: Key offices with functions]**

| German Office | English | What You Do There |
|---------------|---------|-------------------|
| das Bürgeramt / Einwohnermeldeamt | Citizens' office | Anmeldung (registration) |
| die Ausländerbehörde | Foreigners' office | Residence permit, visa extension |
| das Rathaus | Town hall | General city services |
| das Standesamt | Registry office | Marriage, birth certificates |
| das Finanzamt | Tax office | Tax registration |
| die Krankenkasse | Health insurance | Insurance registration |
| die Agentur für Arbeit | Employment agency | Job search, unemployment |

**Narrator:**
> "First stop for every newcomer: das Bürgeramt — for your Anmeldung."
>
> "Most important for foreigners: die Ausländerbehörde — this is where you get your Aufenthaltserlaubnis (residence permit)."
>
> "das Rathaus — the town hall. General city administration."
>
> 🔊 "Wo ist das Bürgeramt?" — Where is the citizens' office?
> 🔊 "Ich brauche einen Termin bei der Ausländerbehörde." — I need an appointment at the foreigners' office.
>
> ⏸️ **[Which office for Anmeldung? → Bürgeramt!]**
>
> *"Ausländerbehörde — foreigners' office. Ithu visa renewal-nu pokkum — every foreigner in Germany goes here!"*

### Scene 4: Making an Appointment (3:00–5:00)
**[SLIDE: Appointment booking interface + phrases]**

**Narrator:**
> "In Germany, you CANNOT just walk into most offices. You need a Termin — an appointment!"
>
> 🔊 "der Termin" — appointment
> 🔊 "einen Termin machen" — to make an appointment
> 🔊 "einen Termin buchen" — to book an appointment (online)
>
> "By phone:"
> 🔊 "Guten Tag! Ich möchte gern einen Termin machen."
> 🔊 "Wann ist der nächste freie Termin?"
> 🔊 "Geht es am Mittwoch um zehn Uhr?"
>
> "Online: Most cities have online booking — search '[City name] Bürgeramt Termin'."
>
> "CRITICAL TIP: Book early! Popular offices have wait times of weeks or months."
>
> *"Germany-il appointment illaathe office-il pokkaan pattilla — advance-il book cheyyuka!"*
>
> ⏸️ **[Practice: Call the Bürgeramt — say you need an appointment]**

### Scene 5: At the Office — The Visit (5:00–7:30)
**[DIALOGUE: At the Bürgeramt]**

**Narrator:**
> "You arrive at the office. Here's how it typically goes:"
>
> **Step 1: Check-in**
> 🔊 "Guten Tag! Ich habe einen Termin um 10 Uhr." — I have an appointment at 10.
> 🔊 "Hier ist mein Ausweis." — Here is my ID.
> 🔊 "Bitte nehmen Sie Platz." — Please have a seat.
>
> **Step 2: Your number is called**
> 🔊 "Nummer 47, bitte!" — Number 47, please!
> (Go to the correct counter/window)
>
> **Step 3: At the counter**
> 🔊 Beamter: "Was kann ich für Sie tun?" — What can I do for you?
> 🔊 You: "Ich möchte mich anmelden." — I'd like to register.
> 🔊 Beamter: "Haben Sie das Formular ausgefüllt?" — Have you filled out the form?
> 🔊 You: "Ja, hier bitte."
> 🔊 Beamter: "Und Ihren Mietvertrag?" — And your rental contract?
> 🔊 You: "Ja, hier."
> 🔊 Beamter: "Gut, einen Moment bitte..."
>
> ⏸️ **[Practice the "You" lines]**

### Scene 7: Survival Phrases for Any Office (9:00–10:30)
**[SLIDE: Phrase list]**

**Narrator:**
> "These phrases work in ANY office situation:"
>
> 🔊 "Ich verstehe nicht. Können Sie das bitte wiederholen?" — I don't understand. Can you repeat that?
> 🔊 "Können Sie bitte langsamer sprechen?" — Can you please speak more slowly?
> 🔊 "Können Sie das bitte aufschreiben?" — Can you please write that down?
> 🔊 "Was brauche ich noch?" — What else do I need?
> 🔊 "Wann bekomme ich das?" — When will I receive it?
> 🔊 "Wo muss ich unterschreiben?" — Where do I sign?
> 🔊 "Vielen Dank für Ihre Hilfe!" — Thank you very much for your help!
>
> "The first three are RESCUE PHRASES. If you don't understand anything, use them!"
>
> ⏸️ **[Memorize the 3 rescue phrases — say them out loud]**
>
> *"Manassilaayillenkil — wiederholen, langsamer, aufschreiben. Moonu rescue phrases!"*

---

### Scene 8: Reading Official Letters (10:30–11:15)
**[SLIDE: Sample official letter structure]**

**Narrator:**
> "You'll receive official letters. Key words to recognize:"
>
> 🔊 "der Bescheid" — official decision/notice
> 🔊 "die Frist" — deadline
> 🔊 "bis zum [date]" — by [date]
> 🔊 "innerhalb von [X] Wochen" — within [X] weeks
> 🔊 "Bitte bringen Sie..." — Please bring...
>
> "ALWAYS check for deadlines (Frist). Missing a Frist can cause real problems!"
>
> "If you don't understand a letter, take it to your Sprachschule teacher or a Beratungsstelle (advice center)."
