# Adipoli German — Intro Video Script

> **Duration**: ~60 seconds
> **Format**: 16:9 (landscape) or 9:16 (vertical reel) — plan for both
> **Audio**: Voiceover (Malayalam/English/Manglish) + background music + German words
> **Visual style**: Pixar-style Kuttan + cinematic Kerala/Germany shots
> **Goal**: Hook → Emotion → Course reveal → Journey preview → "Let's go"

---

## BEAT 1: THE HOOK — Kerala Beauty (0–4s)

**Visual**: Wide aerial/drone-style shot. Lush green Kerala — palm trees, backwaters, morning mist, golden light. Cinematic, breathtaking. No people yet.

**Audio**: Gentle Kerala ambient (birds, water) fading in. Soft music begins.

**Text overlay**: None yet. Let the visual breathe.

**Why**: Beautiful hook that stops the scroll. "This is home."

---

## BEAT 2: MEET KUTTAN — The Dreamer (4–12s)

**Visual A (4-8s)**: Kuttan sitting on the Kerala veranda steps, morning light. Phone in hand. He scrolls past something — stops. His eyes light up. The moment of discovery.

**Visual B (8-12s)**: Close-up of Kuttan's face — curious, excited, a dream forming. He looks up from the phone toward the horizon.

**Voiceover (Malayalam/Manglish)**:
> "Oru divasam... oru notification vannu."
> (One day... a notification came.)

**Or English version**:
> "It started with one moment. One decision."

---

## BEAT 3: THE DREAM — Germany Flashes (12–18s)

**Visual**: Quick montage (1.5s each):
- German city street, winter light, European architecture
- University campus, spring bloom
- Kuttan walking confidently through a German city (flash-forward)
- A Goethe A1 certificate on a desk

**Voiceover**:
> "Germany. University. A new life. But first..."

**Music**: Building energy.

---

## BEAT 4: THE FEAR — "German Is Impossible" (18–22s)

**Visual**: Quick cuts:
- Screen showing complex German grammar (der/die/das)
- Kuttan's confused face
- A German sentence that looks impossibly long
- Kuttan rubbing his face, frustrated (V4 callback)

**Voiceover (Manglish energy)**:
> "German padikkaan... easy alla. Der, Die, Das... enthonnu ariyilla."
> (Learning German... it's not easy. Der, Die, Das... no clue.)

**Or English**:
> "German grammar? Terrifying. Der Die Das? Chaos."

**Why**: Relatable. Every prospective student feels this.

---

## BEAT 5: THE TURN — Enter Adipoli German (22–30s)

**Visual A (22-26s)**: Kuttan picks up his phone. The screen glows — a German learning app (our app). His expression shifts from confused to curious. He taps.

**Visual B (26-30s)**: The phone screen comes alive — colorful, inviting, a lesson beginning. Kuttan's face lights up. This feels different.

**Voiceover**:
> "Pinne njaan kandu — Adipoli German."
> (Then I found — Adipoli German.)

**Or English**:
> "Then I found a course that actually made sense."

**Music**: Shifts to upbeat, warm, confident.

---

## BEAT 6: HOW IT WORKS — Course Montage (30–44s)

The fun part. Quick, engaging cuts showing HOW the course works. Each shot ~2s.

**Visual sequence**:

1. **Phone lesson** (2s): Kuttan on the bus, earphones in, doing a German exercise on his phone. Tapping answers. Gets one right — small smile.

2. **Story-driven** (2s): Phone screen showing Kuttan's animated story — learning words INSIDE a story, not flashcards. (Show the app is immersive.)

3. **Listening practice** (2s): Kuttan on the terrace with earphones, eyes closed, listening to German audio. Nodding along — he understands.

4. **Speaking practice** (2s): Kuttan speaking German to his phone (speech exercise). Concentrated face. The app responds.

5. **Games** (2s): Kuttan playing a German vocabulary game on his phone/tablet. Competitive expression. Fun, not boring.

6. **Anywhere, anytime** (2s): Split-screen or quick cuts — Kuttan studying on bus / in bed / at tea shop / walking. The course goes where you go.

7. **Progress** (2s): Phone showing a progress dashboard — modules completed, streak counter, level up.

**Voiceover**:
> "Stories-il padikkaam. Games-il practice cheyyaam. Bus-il, bed-il, chai kudikkumbol — evideyum."
> (Learn through stories. Practice through games. On the bus, in bed, while drinking chai — anywhere.)

**Or English**:
> "Learn through stories, not flashcards. Practice through games. On your bus. In your bed. With your chai."

---

## BEAT 7: THE JOURNEY PREVIEW — Flash Forward (44–52s)

**Visual**: The emotional payoff. Quick cinematic montage from the series:

1. Kuttan in the classroom — answering confidently (V5)
2. Kuttan at the exam — focused, determined (V6)
3. Fist pump — he passed A1! (V6)
4. Airport goodbye — emotional (V7)
5. Landing in Germany — snow, wonder (V8/V9)
6. Speaking German fluently — at a café, with friends (V10)

**Voiceover**:
> "Kerala-il ninnu Germany-il etthaam. A1... A2... B1. Ella step-ilum njan koode undaakum."
> (From Kerala to Germany. A1... A2... B1. I'll be with you at every step.)

**Or English**:
> "From Kerala to Germany. A1, A2, B1. I'll be with you every step of the way."

**Music**: Peak emotion. Swelling.

---

## BEAT 8: KUTTAN'S PROMISE — Direct to Camera (52–56s)

**Visual**: Kuttan on the Kerala veranda, morning light, golden hour. He looks directly at the camera (fourth wall break). Confident, warm, like a friend. Small nod. The face of someone who's been through the journey and wants to take you along.

**Voiceover (warm, direct)**:
> "Ready aano? Nammuk thudangaam."
> (Are you ready? Let's start.)

**Or English**:
> "Ready? Let's do this."

---

## BEAT 9: CTA + BRANDING (56–60s)

**Visual**: 
- Quick cut to the Adipoli German logo/branding
- Phone mockup showing the app
- "Download now" or "Start free" CTA

**Text overlay**: 
> **Adipoli German**
> Kerala → Germany. Your journey starts now.
> [QR code / link / app store badge]

**Music**: Resolves. Clean ending.
