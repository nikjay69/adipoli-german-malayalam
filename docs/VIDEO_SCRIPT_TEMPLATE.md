## Script Conventions

### Language Mix
- **Primary narration:** English with German phrases
- **Malayalam/Manglish bridges:** Use when they help ("Amma = Mutter — same, right?")
- **German examples:** Always spoken clearly, then translated
- **Pronunciation guides:** Use phonetic hints in parentheses

### Visual Cues
Use these tags in the Visual sections:
- `[SLIDE: title]` — Full-screen text/graphic slide
- `[TABLE: columns]` — Grammar/vocabulary table overlay
- `[DIALOGUE: A/B]` — Split-screen conversation
- `[HIGHLIGHT: word]` — Zoom in on a specific word/ending
- `[FORM: type]` — Show a form being filled out
- `[MAP/DIAGRAM: description]` — Visual aid

### Timing Marks
- `⏸️` — Pause for student practice (3-5 seconds)
- `🔊` — Audio-only moment (student listens and repeats)
- `⚡` — Quick-fire drill segment
- `📝` — Writing prompt

### Difficulty Markers
- `[A1-SAFE]` — Simple pattern, exam-safe to use
- `[A1-STRETCH]` — Slightly above A1, for enrichment
- `[EXAM-TIP]` — Direct exam strategy advice

## Example: Abbreviated Script for Module 18, Lesson 18-1, Video 1

```
# v18-1-1 — Exam Writing Part 1: Form Filling
Module: 18 | Lesson: 18-1 | Duration: 10:00 target
Teaching Goal: Master Schreiben Teil 1 — fill out any German form correctly
A1 Exam Link: Schreiben Teil 1 (50% of writing score)

## Pre-roll (0:00–0:15)
[Course intro]
"Willkommen! Today we tackle the EASIEST points in the entire Goethe exam!"

## Section 1: Hook (0:15–1:30)
### Visual: [SLIDE: "Schreiben Teil 1 = FREE POINTS 🎯"]
### Narration:
"Machane, Schreiben Teil 1 is literally the easiest section of the whole exam.
You get a form. You fill in 5 fields. That's it. 
*Ithu kittiya free marks aanu — miss cheyyalle!*
Let me show you exactly how to ace this every single time."

## Section 2: Core Teaching — The 5 Golden Fields (1:30–5:00)
### Visual: [FORM: Anmeldeformular with fields highlighted one by one]
### Narration:
"Every Goethe A1 form asks for some combination of these fields.
I call them the 5 Golden Fields — learn these and you'll NEVER lose points here.

[HIGHLIGHT: Familienname]
Number 1: Familienname — your family name, your surname.
*Nammude veettu peru — Jayanth, Nair, Menon...*
⏸️ What's YOUR Familienname? Say it now!

[HIGHLIGHT: Vorname]  
Number 2: Vorname — your first name, your given name.
*Nammude swa-ntham peru — Nikhil, Akhil, Lakshmi...*
CAREFUL: Familienname ≠ Vorname! Many students swap these. Don't!

[HIGHLIGHT: Geburtsdatum]
Number 3: Geburtsdatum — date of birth.
CRITICAL: German format is TT.MM.JJJJ — day FIRST, then month, then year.
15th March 1995 = 15.03.1995 — NOT 03/15/1995!
⏸️ Write YOUR Geburtsdatum in German format now!

[HIGHLIGHT: Wohnort / Adresse]
Number 4: Wohnort or Adresse — where you live.
Wohnort = just the city: 'Berlin', 'München', 'Kochi'
Adresse = full address: 'Hauptstraße 15, 10115 Berlin'
[EXAM-TIP] If it says Wohnort, just write the city. Don't overthink it!

[HIGHLIGHT: Telefonnummer / E-Mail]
Number 5: Contact info — Telefonnummer or E-Mail-Adresse
Write your phone number with country code if asked.
E-Mail: write it exactly as it is — nikhil@example.com"

## Section 3: Practice Forms (5:00–8:30)
### Visual: [FORM: Bibliotheksausweis — library card application]
### Narration:
"Let's practice! Here's a Bibliotheksausweis application.
Read the information and fill in each field.
⏸️ Pause the video and try it yourself first!
...
[Walk through each field with the correct answer]"

⚡ Quick-fire: 3 more mini-forms in 2 minutes
[FORM: Sprachkurs-Anmeldung]
[FORM: Arztpraxis form]  
[FORM: Bank account application]

## Section 4: Summary & Exam Tips (8:30–10:00)
### Visual: [SLIDE: Summary card]
### Narration:
"Remember the 5 Golden Fields: Familienname, Vorname, Geburtsdatum, Wohnort, Kontakt.
[EXAM-TIP] Read the form TWICE before writing anything.
[EXAM-TIP] Check: did I put Familienname and Vorname in the right fields?
[EXAM-TIP] Date format: TT.MM.JJJJ — ALWAYS.
*Ithu exam-il miss cheythal kurach silly aayirikkum — so don't!*
Practice 3 forms today and you'll never lose these points.
Bis zum nächsten Mal! Tschüss!"

## Post-roll
[Next lesson teaser: "Next: Schreiben Teil 2 — writing short messages!"]
```

## Checklist Before Marking a Script "Done"

- [ ] Teaching goal clearly stated
- [ ] A1 exam section linked
- [ ] All German examples are grammatically correct
- [ ] Malayalam/Manglish bridges feel natural, not forced
- [ ] Practice pauses included (minimum 3 per video)
- [ ] Key vocabulary overlay cues present
- [ ] Timing is realistic for the target duration
- [ ] Summary section recaps the most important 3 points
- [ ] No A2+ content unless explicitly marked `[A1-STRETCH]`
- [ ] Exam tips marked with `[EXAM-TIP]` where relevant
